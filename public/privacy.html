Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ConcertEventRehearsal handles information when you use the app.

ConcertEventRehearsal

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Overview

Thank you for using ConcertEventRehearsal (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the App.

In general: the App is designed to function primarily without collecting personal data from users.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to help users organize concerts, rehearsals, programs, sheet music, and related event materials.

  • No account required: The App does not require users to create an account or log in.
  • User-provided content: Information you enter into the App, such as concert details, rehearsal schedules, notes, and attached materials, is stored locally on your device.
  • No personal data collection: The App does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or precise location.

3. How Information Is Used

Information entered into the App is used only to provide the App’s core features and functionality, including:

  • Organizing concerts and rehearsals
  • Managing program order
  • Storing and displaying sheet music
  • Supporting rehearsal and playback tools

Data entered into the App remains on your device unless you choose to export, share, or back it up using services available on your own device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Rent or trade user data
  • Share user data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking tools to profile users

5. Data Storage and Security

Data created in the App is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a remote user database for personal information entered into the App.

While reasonable care is taken in the design of the App, you are responsible for securing access to your device and managing your own device backups.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not intentionally integrate third-party services for the purpose of collecting, tracking, or monetizing personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App is designed for organizational and rehearsal use and stores information locally on the device, there is no intentional collection of children’s personal data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.
